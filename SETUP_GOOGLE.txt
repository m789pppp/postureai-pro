الخطوة 1 — Firebase Console
══════════════════════════════════════════════════
1. افتح: https://console.firebase.google.com/project/postureai-prod/authentication/providers
2. دوس على "Google"
3. فعّل Enable
4. حط اسمك في "Project support email"
5. دوس Save

الخطوة 2 — Authorized Domains
══════════════════════════════════════════════════
1. افتح: https://console.firebase.google.com/project/postureai-prod/authentication/settings
2. تحت "Authorized domains" دوس "Add domain"
3. ضيف: localhost
4. دوس Add

خلاص! Google Sign-in هيشتغل.
